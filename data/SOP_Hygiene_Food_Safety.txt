STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
SOP-001: HYGIENE AND FOOD SAFETY PROTOCOLS

Document Version: 2.3
Effective Date: January 15, 2025
Last Updated: October 20, 2025
Department: All Production Areas
Approved By: Head of Operations

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PURPOSE:
To establish comprehensive hygiene and food safety standards for all bakery staff to prevent contamination, ensure product quality, and comply with Dubai Municipality food safety regulations.

SCOPE:
This SOP applies to all employees working in production, packaging, storage, and customer-facing areas.

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PERSONAL HYGIENE REQUIREMENTS:

1. HANDWASHING PROTOCOL
   • Wash hands thoroughly for minimum 20 seconds with antibacterial soap
   • Required before: starting work, after breaks, after handling raw materials, after touching face/hair
   • Use hand sanitizer between washings
   • Keep nails trimmed short and clean (no nail polish allowed)

2. UNIFORM AND PROTECTIVE EQUIPMENT
   • Wear clean bakery uniform daily (white chef coat, apron, pants)
   • Hair must be completely covered with hairnet or chef hat
   • Beard nets required for all facial hair
   • Closed-toe, non-slip shoes mandatory
   • Remove all jewelry except plain wedding band
   • Disposable gloves must be worn when handling ready-to-eat products

3. HEALTH AND ILLNESS POLICY
   • Report any illness, cuts, or infections to supervisor immediately
   • Do not work if experiencing: fever, vomiting, diarrhea, jaundice, open wounds
   • Cover all cuts with waterproof bandage plus glove
   • Medical clearance required before returning to work after foodborne illness

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

FOOD SAFETY PROCEDURES:

1. TEMPERATURE CONTROL
   • Refrigerated storage: 0-4°C (32-39°F)
   • Frozen storage: -18°C (0°F) or below
   • Hot holding: Above 60°C (140°F)
   • Monitor and log temperatures every 2 hours
   • Report any equipment malfunction immediately

2. CROSS-CONTAMINATION PREVENTION
   • Use separate cutting boards for different allergens
   • Color-coded system: Red=meat, Blue=fish, Green=vegetables, Yellow=baked goods
   • Never place finished products near raw ingredients
   • Clean and sanitize all surfaces between tasks
   • Store raw materials below finished products in refrigerators

3. INGREDIENT HANDLING
   • Check all deliveries for quality and temperature
   • Follow FIFO (First In, First Out) rotation
   • Label all opened ingredients with date and time
   • Discard any ingredients past expiration date
   • Store flour, sugar, and dry goods in sealed containers
   • Keep ingredients minimum 15cm off the floor

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CLEANING AND SANITATION SCHEDULE:

CONTINUOUS:
• Wipe spills immediately
• Clean as you go
• Sanitize workstations between tasks

HOURLY:
• Wipe down production counters with approved sanitizer
• Empty trash bins when half-full

DAILY:
• Deep clean all equipment after use
• Mop floors with sanitizing solution
• Clean and sanitize sinks
• Remove all waste to external bins
• Clean display cases inside and out

WEEKLY:
• Deep clean ovens and mixers
• Clean refrigerator/freezer interiors
• Sanitize storage shelving
• Deep clean floor drains

MONTHLY:
• Equipment maintenance and calibration
• Deep clean ventilation hoods and filters
• Pest control inspection

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

APPROVED CLEANING AGENTS:
• Food-safe sanitizer: Quaternary ammonium solution (200ppm)
• Degreaser: Alkaline-based cleaner (for ovens)
• Glass cleaner: Ammonia-free formula
• Floor cleaner: pH-neutral sanitizing solution

MONITORING AND COMPLIANCE:
• Daily hygiene checklist completed by shift supervisor
• Monthly internal audits conducted
• Quarterly Dubai Municipality inspections
• Staff retraining every 6 months
• Violation reporting and corrective action documentation

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

NON-COMPLIANCE CONSEQUENCES:
1st Violation: Verbal warning and retraining
2nd Violation: Written warning
3rd Violation: Suspension pending investigation
Serious Violation: Immediate termination
